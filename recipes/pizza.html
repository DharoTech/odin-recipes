Pizza Recipe



Description

Pizza is a world-famous Italian dish loved for its crispy crust, savory sauce, gooey cheese, and endless topping possibilities. Whether baked in a wood-fired oven or a home kitchen, pizza combines the comforting flavors of bread, tomato, and cheese into one irresistible meal. From a simple Margherita to a fully loaded supreme, pizza is the perfect choice for sharing with family and friends.


Ingredients

  • For the Dough:
    • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 3/4 cup warm water (about 110°F / 45°C)
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • For the Sauce:
    • 1 cup tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 clove garlic, minced
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • For the Toppings:
    • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Sliced pepperoni, vegetables, or other favorite toppings
    • Olive oil (for brushing the crust)
    • Fresh basil (optional, for garnish)

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5–10 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour and salt. Add olive oil and the yeast mixture. Stir until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5–7 minutes, until smooth and elastic. Place in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it preheats.
  5. Punch down the dough and roll it out into your desired pizza shape (round or rectangular).
  6. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the crust.
  7. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top and add your favorite toppings.
  8. Brush the outer edge of the crust lightly with olive oil for a golden finish.
  9. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling.
  10. Remove from the oven, slice, and serve hot. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
